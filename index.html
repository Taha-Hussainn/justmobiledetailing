About Us

Justin Sensbach - Your Car Detailing Expert

Welcome to JUST MOBILE DETAILING, where your vehicle gets the royal treatment it deserves. Founded by Justin Sensbach, a passionate car detailing expert with years of experience, we bring top-quality detailing services right to your doorstep in Baytown, TX.

Our mission is to provide exceptional detailing services that not only enhance your vehicle's appearance but also protect your investment. We use premium products and state-of-the-art techniques to ensure your car looks as good as new.

What sets us apart is our mobile service - we come to you! Whether at your home, office, or any convenient location, our fully equipped mobile unit has everything needed to make your vehicle shine.

Our Services

Interior & Exterior Cleaning

Complete deep cleaning inside and out, removing dirt, dust and grime from every surface for a like-new finish.

Shampooing, Leather & Conditioner

Professional shampoo cleaning for upholstery and carpets plus premium wax application for maximum protection.

Steaming, Waxing and Polishing

Restore your paint's depth and shine with our professional polishing services that remove imperfections. High-temperature steam cleaning kills bacteria and removes deep stains from all interior surfaces.

Ceramic Coating

Premium protective coating that safeguards your paint with long-lasting protection and hydrophobic properties.

Tires, Rims & Door Jambs

Complete cleaning and dressing of tires, rims and often-neglected door jambs for a thorough clean.
